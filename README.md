Adjar is the Google Ads control layer for AI agents. Your account becomes
plain-text config agents work with through the `adjar` CLI — the agent drafts
the change, you review the diff, Adjar applies it safely.

- **Your whole account as plain-text config** — files your agents (and you)
  can read, diff, and edit.
- **Agents draft, you review the diff** — nothing touches your account until
  you approve the change.
- **Applied safely, fully auditable** — every AI-made change is logged and
  reversible.
